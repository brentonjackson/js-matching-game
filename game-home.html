Matching Game

Rules:

User will choose between pairs of 8, 10, or 12 photos. All pictures will be randomly distributed at the beginning of the game.

You are given 3 levels of difficulty: 3, 5, 8 seconds to memorize the locations; then the pictures will be hidden.

Your goal is to match each of the pictures with its copy, clicking the two matching images to reveal them.

The game will be timed. If you complete the game within the timeframe, you win!
